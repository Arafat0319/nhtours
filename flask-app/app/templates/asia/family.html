Plan Family Trip to Asia

(China Edition)

China is wonderfully family-friendly—rich in history, vibrant cultures, striking landscapes, and kid-approved culinary adventures. Below are practical tips on cities, sample itineraries, budgets, hotels, and more, with a focus on China.

How long to go

12–14 days (recommended): The sweet spot for a first family trip to China. You can see the top cultural and natural highlights at a comfortable pace and still enjoy hands-on activities with the kids—without travel fatigue.

3 weeks or longer (go deeper): If time allows, add off-the-beaten-path regions to experience more diverse landscapes and local cultures with your children.

7–9 days (minimum highlights): Short on vacation time? Focus on the essentials in Beijing, Xi'an, and Shanghai to cover China's marquee sights efficiently.
