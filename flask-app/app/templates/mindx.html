NEXUS HORZIONS TOURS

MINDX PROGRAM

The NHTours MindX Program—with MindX symbolizing "exploring the mind and extending learning beyond boundaries"—is designed to transform travel into an immersive educational journey. Created in collaboration with respected educators and industry professionals, the program provides interactive, knowledge-rich materials that connect each destination with key academic insights.

The program supports students in absorbing the information presented by guides while also encouraging independent reflection and review. Each student may take the materials home, leaving them with a lasting record of their learning, as well as meaningful keepsakes that reflect both personal growth and the educational value of the journey.

We have launched our first series of site-based experiential learning programs across Mainland China, including themed routes in Beijing, Xi'an (Shaanxi), Wuhan (Hubei), Quanzhou (Fujian), and Jingdezhen (Jiangxi).
