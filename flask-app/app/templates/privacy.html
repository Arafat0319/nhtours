Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains what information Nexus Horizons Tours ("we," "us," or "our") collects, how we collect it, and how we use and share it. If you have questions or would like more information, contact:

Attn: Privacy Officer
#510–5400 Kingsway, Burnaby, BC
Email: info@nhtours.com
Phone: +1 (778) 882-8565

We may update this Policy from time to time. By providing personal information to us through this website or our services, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

Information About You

What We Collect

As a global travel company, we may collect non-public, personal information directly from you, from people included on your booking, or from your authorized agents. "Personal information" means information that identifies you as an individual, such as:

  • Name and contact details
  • Travel preferences and history
  • Special needs, accessibility requirements, and dietary preferences
  • Information about other people on your booking that you supply to us

We collect personal information when you request information, contact us (and when we contact you), or make a booking.

You are responsible for ensuring that members of your party are aware of this Policy and that you have authority to share their information with us. We will update your information when we can or as required by law to keep it current, accurate, and complete.

Our Use of Personal Information

Providing Services / Legal Requirements

To deliver our services (e.g., flights, tours, insurance), we may process and disclose information outside Canada and the United States. Government authorities at points of departure and/or destination may require us to disclose information for immigration, border control, security, or anti-terrorism purposes. Requirements vary by destination; even where disclosure is not mandatory, we may assist at our discretion.

Other Uses and Disclosures

We use information only for the purposes described at collection and do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information:

  • with your express authorization.
  • to our service providers/agents who perform services for us.
  • as needed to provide our services to you (including contacting you).
  • to entities performing marketing services on our behalf or under joint marketing arrangements.
  • in connection with a corporate transaction (e.g., sale of assets, merger, consolidation, reorganization);
  • for business operations, including customer care, service quality, risk assessment, security, fraud/crime prevention and detection, monitoring, research and analytics, marketing, understanding purchasing trends, dispute resolution, credit checks, and debt collection; or
  • as required or permitted by law.


Contests, Sweepstakes, and Promotions

If you participate in promotions we offer from time to time, we may request information such as your name, email, date of birth, and phone number. We use this information to administer the promotion and may communicate with you (and the contacts you designate) about our products and services. We will not use information you provide about other people for any other purpose without first obtaining their consent.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We do not sell products or services for purchase by children and do not knowingly collect or post information from children under 13 without direct notice to, and verifiable consent from, a parent or legal guardian (as defined by COPPA).

For activities directed to children under 13, we will seek parental permission at the email address provided in the initial request. No child under 13 may submit personal information without verified parental consent. After consenting, a parent may review, correct, or delete the child's information, or withdraw consent. We do not use children's information for marketing or disclose it to third parties for any purpose.

Email

We welcome questions and comments by email. We share messages internally with those best able to respond and retain them until your inquiry is addressed. Messages may be archived for a period or deleted when no longer needed.

Your Rights

  • Access/Correction: Upon request (and as required by law), you may obtain a copy of your personal information and ask us to correct inaccuracies.
  • Marketing Preferences: You may opt out of direct marketing at any time by using the unsubscribe link where available or by contacting us. Please allow a reasonable time to process your request.

Monitoring

To carry out instructions accurately, improve service, and ensure security, we may monitor and/or record: (1) telephone calls; (2) CCTV in and around our premises; and (3) transactions and activities on our website, where permitted by law. All recordings are our property.

Security

Access to personal information collected via our website is restricted to employees and others who need it to provide services or conduct normal business operations. While no website can guarantee security, we maintain appropriate physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards.

Changes to This Policy

We may change this Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on our website and/or made available on request. The most recent version is indicated by the "Last Updated" date below. This Policy does not create contractual or other legal rights on behalf of any party.

Links to Other Websites

For your convenience, our website may link to sites we do not control. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, products, services, or content of those sites.
